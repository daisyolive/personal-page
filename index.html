PhD Student
Department of Landscape Architecture and Urban Planning
Texas A&M University (TAMU)
lideng 'at' tamu.edu

Research interests: Li's research interests focus on the relationship between built environments and human health & well-being, healthy environmental design, and nature-based interventions.

About me: Li Deng is a fourth-year doctoral student in Urban and Regional Science. Li previously worked as the coordinator for the Multisensory Place-Making Lab and the Adaptive Water Urbanism Initiative at Texas A&M University, and now is the study coordinator for the Green Campus Project.
