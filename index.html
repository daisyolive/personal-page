PhD Student
Department of Landscape Architecture and Urban Planning
Texas A&M University (TAMU)
lideng 'at' tamu.edu

Research interests: Li's research interests focus on the relationship between built environments and human health & well-being, healthcare environmental design, and nature-based interventions.

About me: Li Deng is a second-year doctoral student in Urban and Regional Science and the coordinator for the Multisensory Place-Making Lab, the Adaptive Water Urbanism Initiative, and Green Campus Project at Texas A&M University.
